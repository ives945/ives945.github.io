關於博物館

自大地伊始，萬物自有其形象，如此生死覆滅，輪迴百轉， 都在剎那間被收入進這個小小的博物館。你可以隨意選擇自己喜歡的故事，我將帶你重回那段時光。

Since the beginning of the earth, everything has its own image, so life and death are destroyed, reincarnation for hundreds of times, they are all collected into this small museum in an instant. You can choose the story you like at will, and I will take you back to that time.
